Format: JPG
Resolution: 4096 x 2048
Description: Alternate Texture Map of Earth showing it's relief/topography. 

Source images taken from: http://www.ngdc.noaa.gov/mgg/image/2minrelief.html
32 images assembled together as a one 

Copy and paste the next lines into the <earth-altmaps.ssc> file, or create a new one and copy the earth-geoi file into the medres folder:

AltSurface "Topography (Land & Oceans)" "Sol/earth"
{
	Texture "earth-topo.*"
}
